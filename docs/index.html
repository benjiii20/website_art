Khaya connects underrepresented artists with galleries around the world.

We celebrate diverse cultures and help artists present their work globally—while giving galleries a thoughtful, curated way to discover new talent.

Our mission

  • Give artists a platform to be seen and discovered by galleries worldwide.
  • Help galleries discover pre-curated talent that fits their exhibitions—across regions and styles.
