Amara Cole

Lagos, Nigeria • Abstract • Mixed media

Amara builds luminous dreamscapes inspired by West African city lights, blending acrylic, found textiles, and projected light.

Statement

I paint the feeling of neon in rainstorms, remixing fabrics from Balogun Market with bold strokes to preserve memories that refuse to fade. Each piece carries motion—between past and future, between city rhythm and quiet dawn.
